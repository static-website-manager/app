Can I change my monthly subscription? What if I need a larger plan?

Of course! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan whenever you want. Your account will automatically be charged the new plan fee at the beginning of the next month billing cycle.

What happens to my data if I want to cancel my subscription?

Your data is yours, and you can take it with you at anytime! Once you decide to cancel your subscription all of your website data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

What if I need a larger plan?

If you need a larger plan than what is currently offered, simply contact us at support@staticwebsitemanager.com and we’ll work with you to accommodate what we can.

Are there any other limits?

Yes, the maximum allowed website repository size is currently 1GB and the maximum number of supported custom branches is 10. We’re sorry for this barrier and hope to increase limits soon.
