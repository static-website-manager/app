Powerful Features to Help Your Team Collaborate and Publish Static Websites

Access all of Static Website Manager’s features free for 14 days. Get Started

Collaboration Built-in with Git Version Control

Static Website Manager uses git version control to store your website design files. This means it’s easy to coordinate changes with your team members and rollback in the case of a mistake. You can view your whole project history and see who’s made which changes.

Each user in your team gets their own staging branch, and you can optionally give team member’s access to the production branch, custom feature branches, or even other staging branches. Now anyone can make and view changes independently of the production website and without fear of breaking it!

And because it’s your data, you can pull or clone your repository anytime! We’ll event commit form data back into your repository so you’re never locked in.

Simple Blogging and Advanced Content Management

Static Website Manager is the Jekyll CMS you’ve been looking for! Now you can take advantage of the powerful custom design possibilities Jekyll affords while still supporting your team with an easy-to-use web-based interface they already know.

Keep your editor simple with Markdown files or use HTML and take advantage of our easy WYSIWYG editor. Either way our smart Front Matter support provides your team with simple inputs to control advanced features of even the most demanding designs!

Changes are recorded as git commits so your history is always preserved. Need to go back in time? No problem! Plus, every commit triggers a new build of your website so that you can quickly visualize your changes before they go live! And soon we’ll help you squash related changes together before each merge for a more meaninfgul history!

Deploy to the Whole World or just Your World

Every signed-in team member can preview their branches’ websites live through a secure Static Website Manager URL. But when you’re ready to publish your website you can attach a new external deployment!

Attach an Amazon S3 deployment to your (production) branch and your website will be deployed to the specified bucket and updated anytime there’s a new commit!

Need to privately test your website throughout your whole office? Deploy a “preview” branch to a private Amazon S3 Bucket. Static Website Manager can limit your deployment to a private IP address or a CIDR IP address range. And because you’re in control of external deployments, you can setup more advanced features or a CDN to suite your needs.

Animate your Website with Form Responders

Form Responders are a new way to animate your website by commiting data submitted from your live website directly back into your repository! Plus you can include up to ten email addresses and they’ll be sent a copy of each form submission.

Form Responders work across branches! Add comment forms to your production blog that point to the “moderation” branch’s comments data file, then new comments can be flagged before being merged to the production branch.
