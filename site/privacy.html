When you register for Static Website Manager we ask for information such as your name, email address, and credit card information.

Static Website Manager, LLC. uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, billing, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research.

Cookies

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site’s computers and stored on your computer’s hard drive.

Cookies are required to use the Static Website Manager service. We use cookies to record current session information, but do not use permanent cookies.

You are required to re-login to your Static Website Manager account after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents

Although Static Website Manager, LLC. owns the code, databases, and all rights to the Static Website Manager application, you retain all rights to your data.
